# Pazhassiraja College Fine Arts Management System Wiki

Welcome to the official documentation for the **Union Arts Festival Management System**. This wiki serves as a comprehensive guide for administrators, stage managers, and developers involved in the Pazhassiraja College Arts Festival.

---

## 🎭 Project Vision
Our goal is to digitize and streamline the management of arts competitions, ensuring transparency, fairness, and real-time accessibility for all participants and coordinators. From the initial registration to the final certificate generation, every step is powered by a robust, secure, and intuitive digital infrastructure.

## 🛠️ System Overview

The system is built with a **Mobile-First** philosophy and a **Glassmorphic** design aesthetic, leveraging the following powerhouses:

- **Frontend**: Vanilla JavaScript & CSS3 (Advanced Animations)
- **Backend & Hosting**: Firebase (Firestore, Functions, Storage)
- **Security**: Granular Firestore Security Rules & Access Control
