# Portal User Guides

This page provides detailed instructions for the various portals within the **Union Arts Festival Management System**.

---

## 🛡️ Command Center (Super Admin)
**Access:** `coreadmin.html`
The Super Admin portal is the primary control hub for the entire system.

### Key Features:
- **Node Monitoring**: View real-time status of connected administrators and system health.
- **Admin Management**: Whitelist or remove administration emails.
- **Global Settings**: Configure festival titles, academic years, and signature assets.
- **Data Audit**: Periodically scan for inconsistencies in registration records.

---

## 🎭 Stage Manager Portal
**Access:** `stagemanager.html`
Designed for coordinators at the competition venues.

### Workflow:
1. **Live Schedule**: Select your assigned stage to see a real-time list of events.
2. **Digital Lottery**: Draw chest numbers randomly to decide performance order.
3. **Recording**: Use integrated tools to record performance timestamps or capture video (if enabled).
4. **Marks Entry**: Input scores for participants. Note: Scores are marked as 'Pending' until verified by the Chief Superintendent.

---

## 📊 Secretary Hub & Admin Dashboard
**Access:** `admin.html`
The central hub for department secretaries and festival coordinators.

### Tasks:
- **Registration Management**: Approve or modify student registrations for various events.
- **Chest Number Allocation**: Automatically or manually assign chest numbers based on the pre-configured series.
- **Program Scheduling**: Update venue details and timings for individual competitions.

---

## 📝 Student & Appeal Portals
### Student Registration (`student.html`)
- Students can browse events and register.
- Real-time validation ensures students don't register for conflicting events or exceed their participation limit.

### Appeal System (`appeal.html`)
- Students or department heads can submit grievances regarding results.
- **Evidence Upload**: Supports linking to YouTube or direct video uploads to demonstrate performance disputes.
- **Status Tracking**: Monitors the appeal from 'Submitted' to 'Resolved'.

---

## 🏆 Real-time Leaderboard
**Access:** `leaderboard.html`
A public-facing page designed for high-visibility displays.
- Displays department-wise points in real-time.
- Mobile-optimized for students and spectators to check standings on the go.
